An aspiring software engineer.

NEW JERSEY, — I graduated from Rutgers University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and an academic minor in Mathematics. I've always been curious of why and how things worked in most systems. Which is why I am seeking opportunities to learn the responsiblities and software design paradigms of a back end engineer.

Currently, I am a supply chain analyst at Samsung. In the past, I worked as a logistics and accounting assistant at Corentec, a medical implant and device company. In 2018, I competed and won at my first ever hackathon hosted by Publicis.Sapient, where I built an intra-day profit/loss calculation and aggregation back-end module for a hypothetical Global Equities Desk. I am proficient in both C and Java due to much of my coursework concentrating on subjects like operating systems, computer architecture, and programming languages. I am also interested in developing an eclectic background; Currently, I am learning and experimenting with front end tools and technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Furthermore, I enjoy reading books or listening to podcasts pertaining to behavioral economics.

I plan on using this site to showcase my projects and to track my progress when learning new software technologies and languages. More to come.
